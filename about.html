Syed Asif Ali

Founder of Point Media & Pointika · Digital Creator · Entrepreneur

Early Life & Background

Born on 23 January 1997 in South Waziristan Agency, Pakistan, Syed Asif Ali faced challenges like terrorism, lack of resources, and minimal government support.

Despite these hardships, he pursued self-learning in digital media and communications, building expertise without institutional assistance.

Professional Journey

Syed Asif Ali is a UAE-based digital creator and communications professional, focusing on building credible online presence, media visibility, and ethical storytelling.

He has successfully led multiple digital campaigns for high-profile clients, influencers, and public figures, strengthening brand presence across platforms like Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and LinkedIn.

Inspirational Story

From South Waziristan to UAE, Syed Asif Ali transformed personal challenges into global opportunities. Through self-learning, dedication, and digital innovation, he built Point Media and Pointika into recognized digital brands, inspiring young entrepreneurs worldwide.
